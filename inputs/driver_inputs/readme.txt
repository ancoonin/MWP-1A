This folder contains the pre-MWP1A ice sheet extents where the MWP-1A forward sea-level model picks up (14.675 ka)


All files are 512x1024 Gauss-Legendre grids

Laurentide Ice Sheet: LIS_14.675_ka.dat
Greenland Ice Sheet: GIS_14.675_ka.dat
Eurasian Ice Sheet: EIS_14.675_ka.dat
West Antarctic Ice Sheet: WAIS_14.675_ka.dat
Wilkes Land, East Antarctica: WL_14.675_ka.dat

full ICE-7G Ice thickness map: ice_7g_14.675_ka.dat (includes ice that is located outside our five ice masks)

present day topography: etopo2_512_ice5g.dat
